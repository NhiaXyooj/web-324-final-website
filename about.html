Project overview.

The purpose of this project is to rebrand a selected local Milwaukee business, changing their current archetype to a more aligned, strategic archetype. The designer will develop a fresh visual identity that reflects the new archetype and core values, while focusing on modernizing the brands image while staying true to its mission.

Objective: I have chosen Schlitz Audubon which I will be creating a comprehensive brand identity kit. The deliverables will include logos, color palette, typography, and applications across different platforms, culminating in a cohesive brand system.

Project Process: Step 1: Company type, Brand Archetype and Values Select a local business and assign a new archetype. Step 2: Research and Analysis Conduct market research to understand the current brand perception, competitors, and target audience. Considering how a shift in the brand’s tone and archetype could elevate its presence in the community. There will also be similar existing companies, companies with the same archetype and companies with similar values to create the contours.

Analysis

  • In what space does this company exist/operate? Niche?
  • What do they do?
    • What makes it different from the other things like it?
    • Why is it important?
    • Intended platform(s)?
  • Describe the intended audience/user? Who is this for?
    • Empathy Mapping
    • Persona Development
    • Customer Journey
  • What is the company/business passionate about?
    • Mission (purpose)
    • Vision statement (aspirations)
    • Values
    • Positioning Statement
  • What visual attributes come to mind? Describe.

Mood board

Step 3: Fill out Creative Brief After researching, students will complete a creative brief during class with guidance from their instructor. This document will outline the objectives, target audience, brand personality, competitive landscape, and other critical details that will guide the development of the name, visual identity and guidelines.

Step 4: Visual Identity Design & Development Based on the research and analysis, I will begin developing the visual identity. This includes creating the logo picking colors and typography and developing imagery that aligns with the selected archetype and values.

Step 5: Brand Identity Guidelines Design & Development After finalizing the visual identity, students will create a comprehensive brand identity guideline that includes all the elements of the visual identity, along with guidelines on how to use them. This guide will serve as a reference for any future design work related to the brand.

Mock Client Presentation I the designer will present my completed brand identity to a mock client. This presentation will include an explanation of my rational behind the design decisions made, as well as a walkthrough of the different elements of the visual identity.
